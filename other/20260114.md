# 高二英语限时训练十二 词汇与表达精整理

## wing

### ✨ 【音标】 ✨

**英式发音：** /wɪŋ/
**美式发音：** /wɪŋ/

### 🌟 【词性与释义】 🌟

**名词 (n.)**

1. (建筑) 侧翼部分；侧厅；耳房；厢房
   One of the parts of a large building that sticks out from the main part.
2. (鸟、昆虫、飞机等) 翅膀；机翼
   The movable part on a bird or aircraft that enables flight.
3. (政治、组织等) 派别；翼派
   A group within a political party or organization with particular views.

### 🍃 【固定搭配与例句】 🍃

**1. the east/west/north/south wing** （建筑侧翼）

- The east wing houses the library. 东翼楼是图书馆。
- The new wing of the hospital was opened last year. 与医院一侧相连的新楼房去年启用。
- Visitors are not allowed in the west wing during renovations. 翻修期间西翼楼禁止参观。
- The presidential palace has a magnificent south wing. 总统府的南翼楼非常宏伟。
- The museum’s modern art collection is displayed in the north wing. 现代艺术藏品陈列在北翼楼。
- The children’s ward is located in the east wing of the hospital. 儿科病房位于医院东翼楼。

**2. get your wings** （获得飞行资格）

- After months of training, she finally got her wings. 经过数月训练，她终于获得了飞行资格。
- He got his wings in the air force at the age of 22. 他22岁时在空军通过了飞行考试。
- Only the best pilots get their wings from this academy. 只有最优秀的飞行员才能从这所学院获得飞行资格。
- She was proud the day she got her wings. 获得飞行资格那天她非常自豪。
- Many young aviators dream of the moment they get their wings. 许多年轻飞行员都梦想着获得飞行资格的那一刻。

**3. (waiting) in the wings** （准备接替；准备就绪）

- A new leader is waiting in the wings. 新领导已在等待接替。
- Several candidates are in the wings, ready to step forward. 几位候选人已准备就绪，随时可以上台。
- The understudy waited patiently in the wings. 替补演员在后台耐心等待。
- Fresh talent is always waiting in the wings in this industry. 这个行业总有新鲜人才准备接班。
- The next generation of scientists is waiting in the wings. 下一代科学家已蓄势待发。

**4. on a wing and a prayer** （只有一线希望）

- They flew back on a wing and a prayer after engine failure. 引擎故障后，他们仅凭一线希望飞了回来。
- The team won the match on a wing and a prayer. 球队几乎是靠运气赢得了比赛。
- Starting a business in this economy is on a wing and a prayer. 在当前经济下创业只能寄托一线希望。
- We reached the destination on a wing and a prayer. 我们几乎是侥幸抵达目的地的。
- She passed the exam on a wing and a prayer. 她几乎是靠运气通过了考试。

**5. take sb under your wing** （呵护；庇护）

- The experienced teacher took the new student under her wing. 资深老师把新学生置于自己的庇护之下。
- He took the young intern under his wing and mentored her. 他把年轻实习生置于卵翼之下进行指导。
- My aunt took me under her wing when I moved to the city. 我搬到城市后，姑姑一直照顾我。
- The coach took the talented rookie under his wing. 教练特别关照那位有天赋的新人。
- She took the orphaned child under her wing. 她收留并照顾了那个孤儿。

**6. take wing** （展翅飞翔；飞走；想象力自由翱翔）

- The birds took wing at dawn. 鸟儿在黎明时展翅飞翔。
- Her imagination took wing as she wrote the story. 她写故事时发挥了海阔天空的想象。
- Dreams take wing when you believe in them. 梦想在你相信时便会展翅高飞。
- The young eagle finally took wing. 小鹰终于展翅飞翔。
- Ideas take wing in creative minds. 创意在富有想象力的人头脑中自由翱翔。

### 🌿 【词形变化与派生】 🌿

- **winged** (adj.) 有翅膀的；迅速的
- **wingless** (adj.) 无翅的
- **wingspan** (n.) 翼展
- **wingtip** (n.) 翼尖

## drive

### ✨ 【音标】 ✨

**英式发音：** /draɪv/
**美式发音：** /draɪv/

### 🌟 【词性与释义】 🌟

**名词 (n.)**

1. 驱车旅行；驾车路程
   A journey in a car.
2. (also driveway) （从街道通向住宅的）车道
   A private road leading to a house.
3. ~ (for sth) / ~ (to do sth) 有组织的努力；运动
   An organized campaign to achieve something.

### 🍃 【固定搭配与例句】 🍃

**1. go for a drive** （开车兜兜风）

- Let's go for a drive along the coast. 咱们开车沿着海岸兜兜风吧。
- On Sundays we often go for a drive in the countryside. 周日我们常开车去乡间兜风。
- They went for a drive to clear their minds. 他们开车兜风放松心情。
- It’s a pleasant evening—perfect for going for a drive. 今晚天气宜人，非常适合开车兜风。
- We decided to go for a drive instead of staying home. 我们决定开车出去而不是待在家里。

**2. a three-hour drive** （某小时车程）

- It's a three-hour drive to London from here. 从这里到伦敦有三小时的车程。
- The beach is only a two-hour drive away. 海滩仅两小时车程。
- We endured a five-hour drive through the mountains. 我们忍受了五个小时的山路车程。
- The airport is about a one-hour drive from the city center. 机场离市中心大约一小时车程。
- It was a long eight-hour drive, but worth it. 虽然有八小时车程，但很值得。

**3. in/on the drive** （车道上）

- There were two cars parked in/on the drive. 车道上停了两辆汽车。
- Don’t block the drive with your van. 别用货车挡住车道。
- The kids were playing basketball on the drive. 孩子们在车道上打篮球。
- Leaves covered the entire drive after the storm. 暴风雨后车道上满是落叶。
- He parked his new car proudly on the drive. 他自豪地把新车停在车道上。

**4. an economy/export/recruitment drive** （节约/出口/征兵运动）

- The government launched an economy drive to reduce spending. 政府发起节约运动以减少开支。
- Schools organized a recruitment drive for new teachers. 学校组织了招聘新教师的活动。
- Companies joined the export drive to boost sales abroad. 公司参与出口运动以提升海外销量。
- There was a nationwide blood donation drive. 全国开展了献血运动。
- The charity held a fundraising drive that exceeded targets. 慈善机构举办的筹款活动超额完成目标。

**5. sex/energy/drive** （本能驱动力）

- He has incredible drive and ambition. 他有惊人的进取心和抱负。
- Creative drive is essential for artists. 创作动力对艺术家至关重要。
- Lack of drive can hinder career progress. 缺乏驱动力会阻碍职业发展。
- Her competitive drive pushed her to the top. 竞争驱动力让她登上巅峰。
- Young people often have strong natural drive. 年轻人通常拥有强烈的内在驱动力。

### 🌿 【词形变化与派生】 🌿

- **drive** (v.) 驾驶；驱使
- **driver** (n.) 司机
- **driving** (adj./n.) 驾驶的；驱动力
- **driven** (adj.) 有动力的；被驱使的

## be set on sth / doing sth

### ✨ 【音标】 ✨

**关键词发音：** set /set/

### 🌟 【释义】 🌟

**短语动词**
决心要做某事；执意要做某事
To be determined to do or achieve something.

### 🍃 【固定搭配与例句】 🍃

- He’s set on marrying that girl. 他决意要娶那个女孩。
- She’s set on going to university abroad. 她执意要出国读大学。
- They’re set on buying the house despite the price. 尽管价格高，他们仍决心买下那栋房子。
- He was set on becoming a doctor from childhood. 他从小就立志要当医生。
- Once she’s set on something, nothing can change her mind. 一旦她下定决心，什么都无法改变她的主意。
- The team is set on winning the championship this year. 球队决心今年夺冠。
- My parents are set on me studying medicine. 父母执意让我学医。

## find that middle ground

### ✨ 【释义】 ✨

找到折中立场；在争议或冲突中找到双方都能接受的妥协或平衡立场
To reach a compromise acceptable to both sides.

### 🍃 【例句】 🍃

- Parents and teenagers need to find that middle ground on curfew times. 父母和青少年需要在宵禁时间上找到折中立场。
- In negotiations, both parties worked hard to find that middle ground. 谈判中，双方努力寻求平衡点。
- Finding that middle ground between work and family life is challenging. 在工作与家庭生活间找到平衡很具挑战性。
- The committee finally found that middle ground on the budget. 委员会最终在预算问题上达成妥协。
- Good leaders always try to find that middle ground in disputes. 优秀领导总会在争端中寻找折中方案。
- We argued for hours but eventually found that middle ground. 我们争论数小时后终于找到双方都能接受的立场。

## unearth

### ✨ 【音标】 ✨

**英式发音：** /ʌnˈɜːθ/
**美式发音：** /ʌnˈɝːθ/

### 🌟 【词性与释义】 🌟

**动词 (v.)**

1. 挖掘；发掘；使出土 (syn. dig up)
2. （偶然或经搜寻）发现，找到 (syn. discover)

### 🍃 【固定搭配与例句】 🍃

**1. unearth buried treasures / artifacts**

- Archaeologists unearthed buried treasures from the ancient tomb. 考古学家从古墓中挖掘出埋藏的珍宝。
- They unearthed Roman coins during construction. 施工中他们发现了罗马古币。
- The team unearthed a dinosaur skeleton last summer. 团队去年夏天发掘出恐龙骨骼。
- Workers unearthed an old well while digging the foundation. 工人挖地基时发现了一口古井。
- Historians continue to unearth new evidence about the battle. 历史学家不断发掘关于那场战役的新证据。

**2. unearth information / secrets / old diaries**

- I unearthed my old diaries when we moved house. 我们搬家时，我偶然发现了自己的旧日记。
- Journalists unearthed evidence of corruption. 记者们挖出了腐败证据。
- She unearthed some surprising facts about her family history. 她发现了家族史上一些令人惊讶的事实。
- Detectives unearthed crucial documents in the case. 侦探们找到了案件的关键文件。
- Researchers unearthed long-forgotten letters from the author. 研究人员发现了这位作家早已被遗忘的信件。
- The investigation unearthed hidden financial records. 调查揭露了隐藏的财务记录。

### 🌿 【词形变化与派生】 🌿

- **unearthing** (n.) 发掘
- **unearthed** (adj.) 被发掘的

## niche

### ✨ 【音标】 ✨

**英式发音：** /niːʃ/
**美式发音：** /niːʃ/ 或 /nɪtʃ/

### 🌟 【词性与释义】 🌟

**名词 (n.)**

1. 舒适或称心的工作（或生活等）
   A comfortable or suitable role or position.
2. （产品的）商机；市场定位；细分市场
   A specialized market segment with little competition.
3. 壁龛；（山体）凹进的地方
   A shallow recess, especially in a wall.

### 🍃 【固定搭配与例句】 🍃

**1. find/carve out a niche**

- He eventually found his niche in sports journalism. 最后他在体育新闻界找到了理想的位置。
- She carved out a niche as a wedding photographer. 她找到婚纱摄影这个细分市场。
- The company found its niche in eco-friendly products. 公司在环保产品领域找到了定位。
- Many artists struggle to find their niche early on. 许多艺术家早期很难找到自己的定位。
- He carved a niche for himself in children’s literature. 他在儿童文学领域开辟了自己的天地。

**2. a niche market / niche marketing**

- They spotted a niche in the market, with no serious competition. 他们看到市场上一个竞争不激烈的商机。
- Luxury pet accessories have become a profitable niche market. 奢侈宠物配件已成为有利可图的细分市场。
- The development of niche marketing targets specific groups. 针对特定群体的细分营销正在发展。
- Online stores often succeed by serving niche markets. 网店常通过服务细分市场获得成功。
- Vegan cosmetics found a growing niche market. 纯素化妆品找到了不断增长的细分市场。

**3. a niche in the wall / rock**

- There was a small statue in the niche above the door. 门上方壁龛里有一尊小雕像。
- Candles were placed in niches along the cave walls. 洞壁凹处摆放着蜡烛。
- The architect designed beautiful niches for artwork. 建筑师为艺术品设计了精美的壁龛。
- Flowers were arranged in the garden’s rock niches. 花园岩石凹处摆放了鲜花。
- The chapel features marble niches for saints’ statues. 小教堂有大理石壁龛供奉圣像。

## pile in

### ✨ 【释义】 ✨

**短语动词**
（一群人、动物等）迅速挤进、拥入某个地方或车辆
To enter a place or vehicle quickly and in large numbers.

### 🍃 【例句】 🍃

- As soon as the doors opened, everyone piled in. 门一开，大家就迅速挤了进去。
- The children piled in the car excitedly for the trip. 孩子们兴奋地挤进车里准备旅行。
- Fans piled in the stadium hours before the match. 球迷们比赛前几小时就涌入体育场。
- After the concert ended, people piled in the exits. 音乐会结束后，人们蜂拥向出口。
- We all piled in the small elevator at once. 我们一下子全挤进了小电梯。
- The team piled in the bus laughing and cheering. 队员们笑着欢呼着挤上大巴。

# 应用文写作常用句型

## 🌸 表达观点的句型 🌸

- The picture conveys the idea that ... （这幅图传达了……的想法）
  示例：The picture conveys the idea that persistence leads to success.
- The picture reflects the truth that ... （这幅图反映了……的真理）
  示例：The picture reflects the truth that small efforts accumulate into great achievements.
- From the picture, we can clearly see that ... （从图中我们清晰看到……）
  示例：From the picture, we can clearly see that growth requires continuous effort.
- The picture serves as a reminder that ... （这幅图提醒我们……）
  示例：The picture serves as a reminder that we should never give up halfway.
- As is illustrated in the picture, ... （正如图中所示……）
  示例：As is illustrated in the picture, every step forward counts.

### 🌸 总结启示的句型 🌸

- 要相信积累的力量。
  We should believe in the power of small daily efforts.
  示例：We should believe in the power of small daily efforts, for they lead to extraordinary results.
- 成长是一个微妙而渐进的过程，不要轻易放弃。
  Growth is a subtle and gradual process, so we should not give up easily.
  示例：Growth is a subtle and gradual process, so we should not give up easily even when progress seems slow.
- 坚持是看见结果之前最重要的阶段。
  Persistence matters most before results appear.
  示例：Persistence matters most before results appear, as it bridges effort and success.
- 只要不停止，就一直在成长。
  As long as we don’t stop, we are still growing.
  示例：As long as we don’t stop, we are still growing, no matter how small each step may be.
